Discover one of your new five-a-day!

Prickly Pear

Origin: Mexico

Description: The fruit of the Nopales cactus. The fruits can range in color from yellow and light green to orange, pink, and red, depending on the variety and ripeness.

Nutrition: High levels of vitamin C, B-family vitamins, magnesium, potassium, calcium, copper, and fiber.

Uses: Can be eaten raw or dry, and made into various jellies and jams, or alcoholic beverages like vodka.
